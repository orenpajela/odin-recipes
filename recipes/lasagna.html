Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Egg
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Italian seasoning, and salt.
  4. In a baking dish, layer noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  7. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
  8. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
