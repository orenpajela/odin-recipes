Steak

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme)

Instructions

  1. Take the steaks out of the fridge and let them come to room temperature for about 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the grill or skillet over high heat.
  3. Season the steaks generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Add olive oil to the grill or skillet.
  5. Place the steaks on the grill or skillet and sear for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare.
  6. Add garlic and fresh herbs to the pan during the last minute of cooking.
  7. Remove the steaks from the heat and let them rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing.
  8. Serve with your favorite sides.
