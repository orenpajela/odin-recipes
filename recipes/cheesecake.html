Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/2 cup sugar, and melted butter.
  3. Press mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  4. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth.
  5. Add 1 cup sugar and vanilla extract, mix well.
  6. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  7. Add sour cream and flour, mix until smooth.
  8. Pour filling into crust.
  9. Bake for 1 hour or until set.
  10. Let cool, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
