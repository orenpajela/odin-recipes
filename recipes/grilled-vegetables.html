Grilled Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Slice the vegetables into even pieces.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill the vegetables for 5-7 minutes on each side.
  5. Serve hot.
